# No Music Nearby

**The sovereign direct-to-consumer music vault. 882 tracks. Patron-owned files. No streaming, no platform extraction.**

[nomusicnearby.com](https://nomusicnearby.com)

---

## What it is

No Music Nearby is the architectural opt-out from the streaming-platform extraction model. Patrons buy tracks (or buckets of tracks) from the vault and OWN the files. No subscription. No 0.003-cent royalty math. No platform sitting between artist and listener taking a cut and the metadata.

882 tracks across 8 named pricing brackets, all original NoBanks compositions. The Collection tier (501+ tracks at $0.333/track) covers the entire catalog forever.

Built for the people who tried streaming and realized they own none of the music they listen to.

## Stack

Built with TypeScript, Node, Stripe, R2 sovereign storage. Tracks are AAC 320k from 320k MP3 + WAV masters. Patrons download files directly. No DRM, no platform-locked playback, no streaming infrastructure to maintain. Founder licenses available at [nomusicnearby.com](https://nomusicnearby.com).
